About the Conference

Where Technology Meets Sustainability

ICIETS 2027 is the second edition of the International Conference on Innovative and Emerging Technologies for Sustainability, organized by the Department of Computer Science, St. Francis College for Women, Hyderabad, India.

The conference brings together researchers, academicians, industry professionals, and scholars from across the globe to present and discuss cutting-edge research in technologies that drive sustainability, intelligent systems, and next-generation networks.

The inaugural edition, ICIETS 2025, was successfully held in February 2025, with proceedings published in a UGC CARE-listed journal — establishing a strong foundation for this second, more ambitious edition.

ICIETS 2027 will be a hybrid conference, welcoming both in-person attendees in Hyderabad and online participants from around the world. Accepted and presented papers will be published. (Subject to Approval)

Paper Submission

How to Submit

Submissions open soon. Papers must be original and not published elsewhere.

1

Prepare Your Manuscript

Use the official MS Word template. Papers should be 10–15 pages including references.

2

Check Plagiarism

Ensure originality. All submissions are screened using iThenticate before review.

3

Submit via EquinOCS

Submit your paper through online submission system. Link will be provided when submissions open.

4

Double-Blind Review

Each paper is reviewed by at least two independent experts. Remove all author information before submission.

5

Present & Publish

Accepted papers must be presented at the conference to be considered for publication.

Submission Guidelines

Papers must be written in English and follow formatting guidelines

Recommended length: 10–15 pages including figures, tables, and references

Double-blind review — remove all author names and affiliations from the submitted PDF

Simultaneous submission to other venues is not permitted

At least one author of each accepted paper must register and present at the conference

Online presentation is available for international authors

Authors of accepted papers must sign License to Publish agreement
